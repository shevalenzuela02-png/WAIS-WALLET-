Our Mission

To build a generation of financially literate and socially responsible entrepreneurs who harness innovation, transparency, and digital tools to uplift communities, drive inclusive growth, and strengthen the national economy. We aim to empower the youth with the mindset, knowledge, and skills to lead with integrity, solve real-world problems, and create enterprises that deliver both profit and positive social impact.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every young Filipino is financially secure, entrepreneurially equipped, and purpose-driven—capable of creating ethical, sustainable, and impactful enterprises. We envision a nation led by a youth that champions good governance, economic equity, and community empowerment through innovation, collaboration, and socially conscious leadership. In this future, entrepreneurship becomes not just a path to prosperity, but a vehicle for nation-building and systemic change.

Transparency for Good Governance

The purpose of transparency in good business governance is to create trust, take responsibility, and make honest, well informed decisions. It means being open about what’s happening in the company sharing important information with employees, investors, customers, and regulators. When businesses are transparent, people feel respected and included, and it becomes easier to build a fair, ethical, and sustainable environment where everyone can thrive.

Entrepreneur of the Month

Celebrating youth entrepreneurs who champion ethical business and advocacy-driven missions.

Our Entrepreneur of the Month feature highlights students who show outstanding effort, teamwork, and innovation in their business projects. For this month, we are proud to feature Nicole Yang and Kathleen Andrews from Sekolah Ciputra, the founders of Gotcha Matcha. Their business was inspired by their love for matcha and their goal to make authentic Kyoto matcha more accessible and affordable. Through their creativity and dedication, Gotcha Matcha became known for its high-quality ingredients, smooth flavor, and eco-friendly approach. Nicole and Kathleen’s story shows that age is not a limit when it comes to entrepreneurship. With the right mindset, passion, and determination, young people can also build something meaningful that makes an impact in their community.
